Download the App

Install Maricopa Healthy on your phone today — no account, app store or password needed!

NOTE: If you previously downloaded Maricopa Healthy from the App Store or Google Play, that app is no longer supported and should be deleted from your phone. You can then follow the steps below to download the new version. If you experience any issues downloading the app, please contact us at support@maricopahealthy.com for assistance.

iPhone

Step 1

Open the Safari app on your phone and go to: MaricopaHealthy.com/app

Step 2

Tap the blue share icon at the bottom of the screen

Step 3

Scroll down (or swipe across) to find and tap the Add to Home Screen button

Step 4

Tap Add, then go to your home screen and launch the app using the icon

Android

Step 1

Open the Chrome app on your phone and go to: MaricopaHealthy.com/app

Step 2

Tap the Add Maricopa Healthy to Home Screen notification banner

Step 3

Tap Add, then go to your home screen and launch the app using the icon

Step 4

Depending on your device, you may be prompted to tap Add one more time

Who We Are

Maricopa Healthy is a joint project between the Maricopa County Department of Public Health and AZ Health Zone. The app helps people find local farmers markets, delicious recipes and fun ways to stay physically active.

Maricopa County Department of Public Health

We work to make healthy lives possible by protecting the well-being of all county residents and visitors. Some of our work includes:

  • prevention of chronic disease, such as heart disease, cancer, and diabetes;
  • protection against health threats like food-borne illnesses, natural disasters, and preventable injury; and
  • addressing risk factors such as poor nutrition, inadequate activity, and tobacco use.

AZ Health Zone

We support families and communities across Arizona to make healthy changes in homes and neighborhoods. From local gardens in your community, to working with your kids’ schools to create healthy environments, AZ Health Zone provides your family opportunities to be active and healthy your way. Healthy starts here.
